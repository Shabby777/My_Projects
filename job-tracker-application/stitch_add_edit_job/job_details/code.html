subject Job Description

As the Senior Principal Architect at NovaSphere, you will be the architectural anchor for our next-generation cloud ecosystem. You are expected to steer the technical direction of three cross-functional engineering pods, ensuring scalability, security, and developer velocity.

Core Responsibilities

  • Design and oversee the implementation of distributed microservices architecture.
  • Lead technical discovery and due diligence for new product features.
  • Mentor staff engineers and foster a culture of architectural excellence.
  • Collaborate with Product and Executive leadership to align technical strategy with business goals.

The Ideal Candidate

You have a proven track record of scaling platforms to millions of users. You are as comfortable in a whiteboard session as you are reviewing critical PRs or discussing long-term infrastructure budgets.
